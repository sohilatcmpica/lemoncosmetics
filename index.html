Got Questions?

We've got answers.

Is LemonCosmetics suitable for sensitive skin?
Absolutely! Our products are formulated with Cica (Centella Asiatica) and Aloe specifically to soothe sensitive skin. We are 100% alcohol-free and paraben-free.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping is free on orders over ₹999. Standard shipping takes 5-10 business days depending on your location.
Are your products vegan?
Yes, we are 100% Vegan and PETA certified Cruelty-Free. Even our "Snail Mucin" alternative is a plant-based phytomucin extracted from wild yam!
In what order should I use the products?
Easy! 1. Cleanse with the Citrus Cica Cleanser. 2. Apply the Golden Hour Serum on damp skin. 3. Finish with the Invisible Shield SPF (in the morning).
